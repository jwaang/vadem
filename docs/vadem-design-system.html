Design Philosophy

Vadem's design language draws from two worlds: the warmth of a handwritten care note left on a kitchen counter, and the editorial precision of a beautifully organized cookbook. Every element should feel personal, trustworthy, and effortlessly clear — even at 6 AM when a sitter is half-awake looking for the dog food.

Principles

Warm, not clinical. Earthy tones, soft textures, generous radius.
Clear, not clever. Information hierarchy over decoration.
Personal, not corporate. Handwritten accents, pet photos as heroes.
Calm, not urgent. Gentle animations, no red alerts for routine tasks.

Signature Element

The Location Card is Vadem's visual identity. Styled like an instant photo with a handwritten caption, slightly tilted, with a warm shadow. It's the answer to every "where is it?" question — and the thing users will remember and share.

Motion & Animation

Gentle, reassuring, never flashy. Spring easing for interactive elements. Staggered reveals for lists. The check-off animation is the most satisfying moment in the app.

Ease Out
cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)

Cards, modals, page transitions. Decelerating motion that feels natural.

Spring
cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)

Checkboxes, toggles, buttons. Slight overshoot that adds playfulness.

Durations
150 / 250 / 400 / 600ms

Fast for hover, normal for transitions, slow for reveals, 600ms for page load staggers.
